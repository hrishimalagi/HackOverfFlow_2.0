About Hackoverflow 2.0

Launched in 2023 by CSE KLEIT Hubli, Hackoverflow is our annual flagship event and has become the ultimate breeding ground for entrepreneurial spirit and innovation. This year, on November 28th and 29th, Hackoverflow 2.0 returns to set the stage for the biggest offline hackathon in Hubli.

Hackoverflow 1.0

The 2023 edition saw 150+ participants from 30+ colleges, and we're poised to top that in 2024. Hackoverflow isn't just about coding; it's a 24-hour immersion into creativity, collaboration, and entrepreneurship, where your ideas become reality, and innovation knows no bounds. Hackoverflow provides a platform for students to unleash their potential, collaborate with like-minded peers, and transform their entrepreneurial dreams into reality.

What is Hackoverflow?

KLE Institute of Technology presents a two-day National Tech Fest featuring a 24-hour hackathon, tech conferences, and networking. Our vision is to bring together 30 teams from leading Indian engineering colleges, fostering innovation. The event spans 30 hours, including a 24-hour hackathon, providing a platform for participants to showcase their skills.
